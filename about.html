About Our School

Dehra Public School Khabli is committed to quality education, discipline and overall development of students.

Our Journey

Started on 10 November 2005

Dehra Public School Khabli was established with the aim of providing affordable and meaningful education to children from all sections of society. The school is surrounded by calm, green and natural beauty, creating an ideal atmosphere for study and personality development.

We believe in honest education, no false promises and no hidden charges. Our dedicated staff works continuously for academic excellence and character building.
