About Our School

Dehra Public School Khabli was established on 10th November 2005 with a clear aim: to provide quality education in a peaceful, disciplined and student-friendly environment. Our school is located in green and calm surroundings, making it an ideal place for focused learning and overall development.

Why Choose Dehra Public School Khabli?

Strong Academic Performance

Good past performance in board examinations with an excellent overall scoring ratio of classes.

Honest Commitment

No false promises, no hidden charges—only sincere effort and transparent communication.

Dedicated Staff

Committed teachers and staff members who work sincerely for every child’s progress.

Modern Teaching Methods

Academic Excellence

Dehra Public School Khabli has maintained good past performance in board classes. Details regarding past results of our board classes are available online, reflecting our commitment to academic growth and student success.

We also provide online preparation support for board classes and competitive exams through experienced teachers from Kota, Rajasthan, helping students access quality guidance beyond the regular classroom.
